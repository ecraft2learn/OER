Switches

Electrical component that can "make" or "break" an electrical circuit, interrupting the current or diverting it from one conductor to another. It may be operated manually, for example, a light switch or a keyboard button; may be operated by a moving object such as a door, or may be operated by some sensing element for pressure, temperature or flow.

                            

Toggle switch

A toggle switch is manually actuated by a mechanical lever, handle or rocking mechanism. A very common use of this type of switch is to switch lights or other electrical equipment on or off.
