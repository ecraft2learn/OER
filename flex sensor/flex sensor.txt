<h3 style="text-align: justify;">A&nbsp;flex sensor&nbsp;or&nbsp;bend sensor&nbsp;is a&nbsp;sensor&nbsp;that measures the amount&nbsp;of&nbsp;deflection&nbsp;or&nbsp;bending.</h3>
<h3 style="text-align: justify;">Usually, the sensor is stuck to the surface, and resistance of&nbsp;sensor element is varied by bending the surface, which return a resistance value depending&nbsp;the amount of bending.</h3>
