Conductive thread

 

A little thicker than every day polyester or cotton thread, but still thin enough to be sewn by hand in medium-eye needles or with a sewing machine that can handle "heavy" thread.

Because it is strong and smooth, it is ideal for any wearable/e-textile project. Because it is made of stainless steel fibers, it will not oxidize: your projects will not "stop working" because of oxidation after a few months and its safe to wash.

In Conductive Thread - 10 tips you get to know what it´s made of, how it works, what is good for (and not good too). All you need for your project!
