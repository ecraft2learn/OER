Once you are happy with your sliced model, save the fle to an SD card if printing on an Ultimaker 2+ or save onto a USB if printing on an Ultimaker 3. This saves the sliced model as a .GCODE fle which the 3D printer can read. 5. 3D printing To 3D print your model, simply insert the SD card or USB into the front of the Ultimaker. Use the push/rotate button on the front of the printer to select Print, then select your fle. this will start the 3D print. Always watch (or keep checking) the frst layer being printed if possible, If a print is likely to fail, it is usually due to a problem that occurs when printing the frst layer. 6. Post-processing After your model has been 3D printed it may require one or more additional hand fnishing techniques to fnish the model.
