Download it from the App Store, Google Play or from: www.ultimaker.com/app Gently place the printer on its side. Insert the spool holder into the back panel and push until it snaps into place. Connect the cable of the spool holder to the NFC socket. The flat side of the connector faces away from the bottom panel. Guide the cable through the cut-out in the back panel. Any remaining cable can be coiled up against the back panel. 3. Install the printer Insert the cable cover into the back panel to keep the cable in place. Gently place the printer upright again. Open the front build plate clamps to insert the glass plate. Make sure the glass plate slides into the rear clamps and close the front clamps. Connect the power supply to a wall socket and connect it to the printer. The flat side of the connector faces downwards.
