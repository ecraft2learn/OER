3D Printing in the Classroom This half day, professional development course for up to 10 teachers equips you with everything you need to be able to develop, deliver and manage 3D printing projects with your students. The course includes: • • • • • • • • • • Overview of the 3D printing process. 3D design tools, techniques and selecting appropriate 3D modelling software. How to utilise and embed 3D printing within your subject area(s). How to develop and progress pupils skills in 3D design and printing. Age/stage specific 3D printing skills and projects. How to develop a successful 3D printing project. How to manage the 3D printing workflow in an educational environment. How to deal with technical issues and access support. Budgeting for and managing ongoing running costs. Where to access classroom resources and 3D files.
