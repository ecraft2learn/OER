13 Unboxing The Ultimaker comes in reusable, durable packaging, specially designed to protect your Ultimaker 3. Unpacking Follow the steps below properly to unpack your Ultimaker 3. 1. 2. Loosen the strap by opening the buckle. 3. Remove the top part of the styrofoam packaging with the two spools of filament and factory test print in it. 4. Take out the accessory box. 5. Remove the styrofoam part that is placed at the front bottom side of the Ultimaker 3. 6. Take the Ultimaker 3, remove the bottom styrofoam part and place the Ultimaker 3 on a flat surface. 7. Cut the zip tie that secures the print head. 8. Remove the piece of tape from the print head. Take the Ultimaker 3 out of the cardboard box.
